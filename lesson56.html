Lessons 5 and 6

In lesson 5, we learn HTML forms. We learn the form fields, different boxes, and controls.
At the end of lesson 5, the student will make a submission sheet with the following requirements:

1. Heading
2. Single Text Lines
3. Date Select
4. Submit Button

Link to Chapter 5

In lesson 6, we get an introduction to CSS. We learn what CSS is, implement it 3 different ways into our HTML, and get a beginner's look at the language.
At the end of lesson 6, the student will make a homework assignment with the following requirements:

1. HTML: Heading and list with links
2. CSS: H1, .list-item

Link to Chapter 6
After lessons 5 and 6, the students get work time on the homework assignments and do the quiz. After this day is when the midterm is introduced and is due the next day.
